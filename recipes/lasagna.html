Lasagna

Description

This is dish is one of the most exquisite dishes of lasagna available on internet. Follow the next steps carefully and you will soon be serving 3-5 people a scrumptious meal.

  • Prep time 30 min
  • Cook time 60 min

Ingredients

  • 2 feet of lasagna noodles
  • 1 can of spaghetti
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Get a glass sheet that is 12 in X 8 in
  2. Place 2 rows of noodles in the pan
  3. Rub butter on the noodles
  4. Place sauce and meat on the noodles
  5. Repeat steps 2 - 4 until you fill the sheet to the top
  6. Preheat oven to 350 and cook for 1 hour
  7. After cooking let cool for 10 minutes then cut into rectangles
  8. Enjoy!
