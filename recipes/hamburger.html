Hamburger

Description

This is one of the finest hamburger recipes available on the internet. Cook this and you will have a flavorful burger that is bursting with juices.

  • Prep time 10 min
  • Cook time 20 min

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger paddies
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 onion
  • Condiments of your choice

Steps

  1. Get a pan and place you 4 patties in the pan
  2. Place butter in pan and cook patties at medium heat for 5 minutes then flip
  3. Let cook for 5 minutes then flip again
  4. Repeat 2 more times
  5. Take burgers of the stove place in bun and add lettuce, onions and the condiments of your choice
  6. Enjoy!
